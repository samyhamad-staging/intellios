Key observations: (a) the top-level DB query at lines 52–78 IS wrapped in try/catch and logs `cron.webhook-retries.query_failed`, (b) `runCronBatch` has per-item try/catch so anything inside `processDelivery` (including `decryptSecret`) cannot produce a route-level 500, (c) the only un-guarded paths post-query are `recentFailedItemIds` (reads `cron_item_failures`) and `openRun` inside `runCronBatch` (INSERTs into `cron_runs`).
