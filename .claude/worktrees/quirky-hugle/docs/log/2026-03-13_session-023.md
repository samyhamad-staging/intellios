Phase 20: Regulatory Intelligence & Version Audit Trail. ## Decisions

- **Stateless compute for regulatory assessments and diffs** — no new tables, no stored state. Assessments are always current; historical diffs are reproducible from immutable ABP records.
- **Significance model is semantic, not semver** — major = governance policies changed (what a reviewer cares most about), minor = capabilities/constraints, patch = identity/metadata.
- **Policy templates create real enterprise rows** — importable as editable first-class policies, not read-only references. Each creation is audited.
- **countGaps helper simplified** — `evidenceStatus === "missing"` is the correct sole condition; the `!== "not_applicable"` guard was logically redundant.
