## FTE Reduction Breakdown

### Jobs Eliminated (Redeployed)

**7 FTE positions can be:**

1. **Redeployed within MRM team** (recommended):
   - Policy engineers (2 FTE) → Governance policy refinement, advanced policy composition design
   - Monitoring specialists (2 FTE) → Drift detection tuning, advanced analytics
   - Analysts (3 FTE) → High-risk exception handling, remediation work

2. **Redeployed to other functions**:
   - ML infrastructure team (2-3 FTE)
   - Risk management (2-3 FTE)
   - Compliance (1-2 FTE)

3. **Eliminated (natural attrition)** (recommended if organization downsizing):
   - Documentation specialists (2 FTE) → Role naturally reduced; don't backfill
   - Analysts (1-2 FTE) → Through attrition; offer severance packages for transition

**Recommendation:** Redeploy rather than eliminate. Freed FTE can focus on higher-value governance work (policy innovation, advanced analytics).
