## How to Use the Glossary

The glossary is organized alphabetically and includes:

- **Term name** — The canonical term as used throughout the knowledge base
- **Definition** — Concise explanation of the concept
- **Context** — Where and how the term is used
- **Related terms** — Other terms that clarify or relate to this concept
- **See also** — Links to relevant articles and sections
